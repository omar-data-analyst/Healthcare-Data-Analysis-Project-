# Steps to modify data before the analysis process
1. **I added a conditional column called “Age Group”**
   to determine the age group of the case according to the number of years it is old.

   _This is to facilitate the analysis process and understand which age groups are the most sick and which diseases are affected and
    tounderstand whether there is a relationship between increasing the age of the case and the number of cases and other analyses._

2. **I added a column called “Duration”**

   _to calculate the duration of the case’s stay in the health facility (in days) to study the different relationships with the duration of     the case’s stay and what is the average duration for each disease case._

3. **I added a column for the "year" Column**

   _the Year with the disease case in order to make comparisons in the number of cases in each year and develop a time chart for the rate of    cases as well as the budget spent during each year by health insurance companies._

#What did you do to visualize the data?

**I made two Dashboard**

**One for financial analysis**

It deals with graphs that study the annual spending rate of insurance companies
The most companies in terms of total spending amounts and total financial spending for each disease and others
This is to answer various questions, the most common of which are:
1. Is annual spending increasing or decreasing?
2. Is the rate of spending in sync with the rate of disease cases, that is, are insurance companies always committed over time?
3. What are the medical conditions that drain the insurance companies’ economy the most?

**The other Dashboard for statistical analysis of disease cases**

It deals with graphs for a study 
The time rate of cases - the most receiving hospitals - the relationship between the treatment used and the case rate - the percentage of admissions for different cases (emergency - detention - urgent) and others. 
This is to answer various questions, the most common of which are:
1. Does the case rate increase or decrease over time?
2. Is there a treatment that has a noticeable effect on the rate of a particular condition?
3. What are the diseases most commonly found in cases?
And other questions
